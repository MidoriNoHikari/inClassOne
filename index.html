Sword Art Online:
Set in futuristic Japan, where virtual reality video games are taking over the markets, it follows the lives of Kirito and his friends as they realize that they are trapped inside of the latest game, Sword Art Online. After being informed that dying in the game means dying in reality, many players wonder if they'll ever make it home.

Paragon:
Paragon is not your typical MOBA (Massive Online Battle Arena) like League of Legends or DOTA2. This game, currently in it's beta-testing phase, offers the strategy of the usual MOBA combined with all of the thrill of a 3rd-Person Shooter. While I can't say much more since it is still in beta, you should check it out at www.epicgames.com/paragon

Sabre Fencing:
No, not the fencing you use to mark the boundaries of your yard or keep out pesky neighbors, but the lightning fast, blade sweeping action only seen by Olympic athletes. Find a local club and get started today! Featuring swift swords and daring moves, once you start, you'll never want to stop.
